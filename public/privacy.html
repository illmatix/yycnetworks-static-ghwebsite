Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-07

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: When you submit our contact form, we receive the fields you enter (e.g., name, email, message contents, interest type).
  • Communications: Emails you send to us, and our replies.

How we use information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information with service providers only as necessary to operate the site and respond to you, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Cookies and tracking

International transfers

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described (e.g., responding to inquiries, ongoing client relationships), and to meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When no longer needed, we aim to securely delete or anonymize it.

Security
